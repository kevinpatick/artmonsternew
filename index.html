About the studio

What sets Art Monster apart.

  • Vast Expertise
  • Attention to Detail
  • Wide Variety

Every artist at Art Monster is fully certified, health‑department compliant, and trained on cutting-edge equipment and sterilization standards.

Years of tattooing in St. Louis mean we’ve seen almost every skin type, placement, and style, which helps them make smart calls on design, sizing, and aftercare.

Pieces are built slowly and intentionally, from first sketch to final wipe. Line weight, contrast, and negative space are planned so the design stays readable long‑term.

Artists obsess over small decisions — from stencil placement to how shading wraps around muscle and bone — so the finished tattoo looks balanced from every angle.

The crew handles a wide range of tattoo styles: black & gray realism, neo‑traditional, watercolor, Trash Polka, geometric work, script, and fully saturated color pieces.

Whether you want a subtle fine‑line script, a bold color sleeve, or an intricate pattern build, there’s an artist who can translate the idea into a cohesive design.

Services

Tattoo services built around you.

Whether it’s your first tattoo or another addition to a full sleeve, the goal is the same: clean work that fits your story and heals right.

Custom tattoo designs

Bring a loose idea, a few reference photos, or just the story behind the piece. Our artists translate that into a custom design that fits your body, style, and long‑term plans.

Professional tattoo services

From small single‑session pieces to larger multi‑session projects, we use professional‑grade equipment, premium inks, and clean technique for consistent, high‑quality tattoos.

Cover‑up & repair

Not every tattoo can be erased, but many can be reworked. We specialize in cover‑ups and touch‑ups that use smart design, line weight, and shading to revive old or unwanted ink.
